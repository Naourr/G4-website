Birds

birds are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by feathers, beaks, and the ability to lay eggs.

Featured Bird

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their excellent eyesight, allowing them to spot prey from great distances while flying high in the sky.

Common birds

Parrot

Parrots are highly intelligent birds and are known for their ability to mimic human speech and sounds.

Owl

Owls are nocturnal birds with exceptional night vision and silent flight, making them effective hunters.

Penguin

Penguins cannot fly, but they are excellent swimmers adapted to life in cold ocean environments.

Peacock

Peacocks are famous for their colorful tail feathers, which they display to attract mates.

Hummingbird

Hummingbirds can flap their wings up to 80 times per second and are the only birds that can fly backward.

Sparrow

Sparrows are small, social birds commonly found near human settlements all over the world.

Flamingo

Flamingos get their pink color from pigments found in the algae and shrimp they eat.

Woodpecker

Woodpeckers use their strong beaks to drill into trees in search of insects and to create nesting holes.

Swan

Swans are graceful birds known for forming long-term pair bonds with their mates.
