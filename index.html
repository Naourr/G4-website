Animal Fun Facts by Species.

This site shares interesting and educational facts about different animal species. Explore mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fish to learn what makes each group unique, fascinating, and important in the natural world.

Mammals

Warm-blooded animals with hair or fur that feed their young with milk.

Birds

Feathered, warm-blooded animals that lay eggs and often fly.

Reptiles

Cold-blooded, scaly animals that usually lay eggs on land.

Amphibians

Cold-blooded animals that live both in water and on land during their life cycle.

Fish

Cold-blooded, aquatic animals that breathe through gills.
